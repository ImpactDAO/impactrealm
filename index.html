SANDBOX MMORPG

In Ember Sword, what you do and when is entirely up to you! Being mechanically strong & knowledgable will net you many victories on the moon of Thanabus.

END-GAME CONTENT

Ember Sword is built around the philosophy of "gameplay first" and will feature challenging end-game PVE, hardcore PvP, a living economy, and much more!

IMMERSIVE STORYLINE

Thanabus has a deep story to be told. You'll face tough challenges, meet ruthless characters, and make new friends when you set foot on this moon.

PROFESSIONS AND SKILLS

Progression in Ember Sword is based on advancing your skills from level 0 to 100. Each weapon type has its own associated skill and so do other RPG elements like crafting.

TERRITORIES AND LANDS

The moon of Thanabus is split into four nations. Each nation has three types of territories, each posing a different challenge: Kingship, Wilderness, and Outlaw.

CARAVAN TRADING

Acquire valuable resources unique to your home region, and sell them by launching a caravan across the dangerous Outlaw Territories. Be Careful with the wagon!

LAND OWNERSHIP

The first land sale of Ember Sword takes place in Solarwood - one of the four nations of Thanabus. The sale features four types of land: regular plots, settlements, and towns.

ARTIFICIAL GAMEMASTER

In Ember Sword, the world is alive and populated with real players. The AI gamemaster utilizes the players around you to build new experiences every time you play.

Federal Territory

The foundation of land in Ember Sword revolves around square 1x1 plots that are, roughly, the size of one screen. Regular plots are the base form of land in the game, your chance to own a piece of Thanabus. Regular plots come with the Sir/Lady title and have traits that are unveiled as the game world unfolds, which can include campsites, NPC houses, resource deposits, monster spawns and more!
